# Business and Marketing Strategy Task

## Project Overview

This project is completed as part of the CodeAlpha Business and Marketing Strategy Internship. The project focuses on understanding business planning, market analysis, customer behavior, and developing effective marketing strategies.

The main objective of this task is to analyze business challenges, identify market opportunities, and create strategic solutions that help improve brand growth, customer engagement, and business performance.

## About Business and Marketing Strategy

Business strategy defines the long-term goals and direction of an organization, while marketing strategy focuses on promoting products or services, reaching target customers, and increasing market presence.

A strong marketing strategy helps businesses understand customer needs, analyze competitors, and develop effective campaigns for growth.
