# Market Research Report - Starbucks Corporation

## Project Overview

This project is completed as part of the CodeAlpha Business and Marketing Strategy Internship. The project focuses on conducting a market research analysis of Starbucks Corporation to understand its business model, market position, customer behavior, competitive environment, and marketing strategies.

The main objective of this report is to analyze Starbucks' presence in the global coffee industry and evaluate the strategies that contribute to its brand success.

## About Starbucks Corporation

Starbucks Corporation is a global coffeehouse company known for providing premium coffee products, beverages, food items, and customer experiences.

The company operates thousands of stores worldwide and has established itself as one of the leading brands in the global coffee market through strong branding, customer loyalty programs, and innovative product offerings.

## Market Analysis

The global coffee industry has experienced significant growth due to increasing coffee consumption, changing lifestyles, and demand for premium beverages.

Customers today prefer coffee shops not only for beverages but also as social and working spaces. Starbucks has successfully positioned itself as a lifestyle brand rather than just a coffee provider.
